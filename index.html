What is ProdOps?

ProdOps is the concept of measuring everything through the application development lifecycle. ProdOps is the missing component from requirements definition to application monitoring to ensure the application features meet your targeted outcomes. ProdOps enhances a lot of the current methodologies you leverage today.

  • Agile
  • Scrum
  • Lean
  • Kanban
  • 12 Factor
  • Microservices
  • Continuous Integration
  • Continuous Delivery
  • DevOps
  • ChatOps

The methodologies above describe the workflow for how source code could move throughout a company and eventually make it to a user on their computer, tablet, or phone.

ProdOps Values

Product Management

Accuracy

Prioritize features to align with objectives and establish metrics for validation.

Product Engineering

Utilization

Continuous alignment of development resources to maximize productive capacity.

Performance Engineering

Quality

Ensure non-functional performance targets (throughput, latency, etc) are met and improving.

Infrastructure Engineering

Efficiency

Measure the improvement of production cycles (time, risk, etc) through repeatable and automated deployment processes.
